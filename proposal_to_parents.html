Through this program, your child will:

  • Build focus, discipline, and creativity
  • Learn to play musical instruments professionally
  • Gain exposure to international music standards
  • Prepare for globally recognized exams like ABRSM

Your child can learn:

  • Piano
  • Violin
  • Guitar
  • Recorder
  • Voice Training (Singing)
  • Music Theory

Structured Learning Path

The program is carefully designed in three levels:

  • Beginner
  • Intermediate
  • Advanced
At each level, your child will receive:
  • Step-by-step guidance
  • Practical playing skills

  • Continuous assessment and progress tracking

At the end of each session, your child will perform in:
