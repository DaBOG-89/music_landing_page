About Me

I am a passionate and dedicated Music Instructor with experience teaching music theory, piano, violin, guitar, recorder, and voice to students of different ages and skill levels. My goal is to help each student develop not only technical ability, but also a deep understanding and appreciation of music.

I believe music education should be structured, engaging, and inspiring. Whether I am introducing beginners to the basics of rhythm and pitch, or guiding intermediate students in technique and expression, I tailor my teaching approach to suit individual learning styles. My lessons balance theory and practice, ensuring students understand what they play and play what they understand.

I place strong emphasis on music theory as the foundation for confident performance, sight-reading, and musical creativity. Through practical examples on the piano, violin, guitar, recorder, and voice, I help students connect theoretical concepts to real musical situations.

As a multi-instrumentalist, I am able to guide students across different instruments, helping them build proper technique, good posture, accurate intonation, and expressive performance. I also encourage ear training, discipline, and musical sensitivity, which are essential skills for long-term growth.

Above all, I am committed to nurturing confidence, creativity, and a lifelong love for music in every student I teach.
